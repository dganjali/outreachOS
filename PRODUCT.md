# PRODUCT.md - OutreachOS

register: product

## What it is
Agent-powered cold-outreach platform. One mission in (mode + offer + audience),
a reviewable pipeline out: ranked targets, verified contacts, sourced evidence,
personalized email drafts, sent from the user's Gmail with replies routed back
and auto-classified. Modes: sponsorship, BD, internship, recruiting, sales.

## Users
Founders, SDRs, and solo operators running cold outreach themselves. They live
in the app during focused work hours, mid-task, deciding what to send. They are
fluent in tools like Linear, Notion, and Stripe's dashboard; they trust earned
familiarity and distrust anything that feels off. They are time-poor: the next
action must be obvious.

## Product purpose
Replace the manual tool-hopping dance (Apollo → LinkedIn → ChatGPT → Sheets →
Gmail) with one mission-in / pipeline-out flow. The agent does the legwork; the
human reviews and sends. Every output is reviewable before it leaves the inbox.

## Voice and tone
Plain, direct, confident. Speak to an operator, not a beginner. Name the next
action. No hype, no exclamation marks by reflex, no "AI magic" language. Status
copy is honest (Suggested / Researching / Drafted / Sent / Replied). Empty
states teach the loop, they don't just say "nothing here."

## Strategic principles
- Action-first: every screen surfaces the next step in the launch → review →
  send loop. The dashboard leads with what needs the user now, not vanity stats.
- Reviewable, never autonomous-by-surprise: the user approves before send.
- Trust through familiarity: standard navigation, predictable structure,
  consistent affordances. Delight lives in moments (a clean run view, a warm
  empty state), not in reinvented controls.

## Anti-references (what this must NOT look like)
- The cool-blue / crisp-white / geometric-sans outreach-SaaS template
  (Apollo, Outreach.io, Salesloft). That is the first-reflex look; we reject it.
- Generic Tailwind status chips (blue info, candy green/red) bolted onto a warm
  surface. That mismatch is exactly the incohesion the rehaul removed.
- Hero-metric dashboards: big number, small label, gradient accent, repeat.

## Identity
Editorial warm-paper + deep forest green. Fraunces (display serif) for headings
and large numbers; Inter for UI and body. Light theme: the app is used at a
sunlit desk during the workday. See DESIGN.md for the system.
